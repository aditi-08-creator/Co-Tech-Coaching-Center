About CoTech Coaching Center

CoTech Coaching Center is a well-known coaching institute running successfully since the last 25 years in Prayagraj. We believe in providing all deserving students with the best curriculum and pedagogy along with the highest quality of education in coaching for all classes (all boards) and entrance exams (NEET/JEE).

CoTech brings out the latent strengths of every student so that they feel motivated and applauded for their strengths and explore the greater depth of their potential.

Our Team: CCC has a team of highly qualified and competent teachers comprising experienced faculties, graduates, and post-graduates from top-grade universities.

Small Batch Size: Our small batch size helps each student with a large scope for fruitful, one-to-one interaction between student and teacher.

Flexible Timings: Morning and Evening Batches are provided considering the comfort of students and parents regarding school/college timings and availability of transportation.
