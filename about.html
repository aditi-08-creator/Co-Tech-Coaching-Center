About Us

CoTech Coaching Center a well known, coaching institute running successfully since last 25 years in Prayagraj. We believe in providing all deserving students in Prayagraj with the best curriculum and pedagogy along with the highest quality of education in coaching for all classes(all boards) and entrance exams(NEET/JEE)

CoTech Coaching Center brings out of the latent strengths of every student so that they feel motivated and applauded for their strengths and explore the greater depth of their potential.

CCC is not just another coaching class, we believe in teamwork and our aim is to provide excellent training in all subjects i.e, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer and English to the level of school and All India Level Entrance Exams. CCC has team of highly qualified and competent teachers comprising of experienced faculties, graduates and post-graduates from top grade universities.

Our small batch size helps each student and here is a large scope to a fruitful, one to one interaction between student and teacher.

Morning and Evening Batches are provided considering the comfort of students and parents regarding School/college timings availability of transportation etc.

The founder "Dhananjay Sharma" stands for education for every child irrespective of it's economic status.

Students prefer CCC as it is the combination of unique features of the programme, dedication of our faculty, child centric approach from the management, understanding the needs of the students and last but not the least consistent results throughout the years.
